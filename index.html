Welcome to Type-Trype!

In this game, you will be presented with a randomized text. The goal is to type the words correctly. Spaces are automatically ignored therefore you do not have to use the Space button. The score is determined on how quickly you finish the texts and your score is shown at the end. Use the keyboard image as a reference to make learn to type faster!
